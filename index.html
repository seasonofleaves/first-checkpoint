Jeremy Clarkson is an English broadcaster, journalist, and writer who specializes in motoring. He is best known for his role on the BBC TV show Top Gear, along with co-presenters Richard Hammond and James May.

Awards and Accomplishments

Clarkson was a host for Top Gear from 1988-2015, and is considered one of the most influential people in the motoring industry. While on Top Gear, Clarkson and co-host James May became the first people to drive a motor vehicle to the north pole. Following his exit from Top Gear, Clarkson (along with James May and Richard Hammond) moved on to Amazon Prime Video to continue their adventures with The Grand Tour. In 2020, Clarkson began a new show on Amazon, Clarkson's Farm. In 2021, he and co-farmer, Caleb Cooper, won the British Farming Award for Flying the Flag for British Agriculture. Clarkson has written many books, including a series based on his time on the farm. (Pictured left)
